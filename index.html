Hello, my name is Benedikt Bramböck and I am a Berlin-based type designer. I work in font technology, workflow optimization and knowledge transfer. Previously I worked at Fontshop International and Alphabet Type. I occasionally write about typefaces (Trianon, Totentanz, BC Brief) at Typographica. I curate and organize events (Typostammtisch Berlin from 2015 to 2020, Berlin Type School Away Days conference in 2016). I rarely update my website, but you can follow me on Twitter, Instagram, Github or contact me via email.
