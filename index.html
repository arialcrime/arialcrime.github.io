Hello there, I am Benedikt Bramböck, a Berlin-based type designer. I take care of fonts, workflows and knowledge transfer at Alphabet Type. I write about typefaces (Trianon, Totentanz, BC Brief) at Typographica. From 2015 to 2020 I curated and organized events at Typostammtisch Berlin. In 2016 I helped organize an independent type conference. There is no portfolio here, but you can follow me on Twitter, Instagram, Github or simply contact me through old-fashioned email.
